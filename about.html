We are a group of creatives who are working to free data from network drives, databases, and USB sticks. Our goal is to connect people with not just data, but to provide the confidence in data that enables data-driven decision making.
Based in Melbourne, Australia, we are an international group of software developers. Our core software expertise includes Cyber Security, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, and Systems Development.
In prior lives, we have worked as Project Engineers, Industrial Engineers, Analysts, and as Quality Assurance Officers. We are combining this industry experience with software and computing expertise to create a unique data communication solution.
We are developing this solution with support from The University of Melbourne's Innovation Project program, placing us uniquely to leverage the experience of academic staff and artificial intelligence expertise.
